<p><ul><li>UV Type</li><li>Non-UV Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer grinding tapes market comprises two main types: UV and non-UV tapes. UV type tapes utilize ultraviolet light to facilitate easier release from the wafer after grinding, enhancing efficiency and reducing damage to delicate surfaces. In contrast, non-UV type tapes do not require light activation and are often used in applications where simplicity and cost-effectiveness are prioritized. Both types serve critical roles in semiconductor manufacturing, influencing the quality and precision of the grinding process.</p></p>
