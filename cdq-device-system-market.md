<p><p>The CDQ (Coke Dry Quenching) Device System market encompasses several key components critical for efficient coke production and energy recovery. The Red Coke Loading System facilitates the transfer of coke to cooling units, while the Cold Coke Discharge System efficiently unloads cooled coke. The Gas Circulation System manages flue gas for optimal heat recovery. The Boiler System generates steam, the Water Treatment System ensures water quality, and the Dry Quenching Furnace allows for effective coke cooling. The Air Supply Device maintains necessary airflow throughout the process.</p></p>
