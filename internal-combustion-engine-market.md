<p><p>The internal combustion engine market is primarily divided into two types: 2-stroke and 4-stroke engines. </p><p>2-stroke engines complete a power cycle in two strokes of the piston, delivering high power-to-weight ratios and simpler designs, making them popular in smaller applications like chainsaws and mopeds. </p><p>In contrast, 4-stroke engines complete their cycle in four strokes, offering better fuel efficiency, lower emissions, and longer lifespan, making them widely used in automobiles and larger machinery. Each type serves distinct market needs and applications.</p></p>
