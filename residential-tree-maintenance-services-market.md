<p><p>Residential tree maintenance services are categorized into two main market types: corrective and preventive maintenance. Corrective maintenance involves addressing existing tree issues such as pruning damaged branches or treating diseases. This type is reactive, focusing on restoring tree health and safety. In contrast, preventive maintenance emphasizes proactive measures, such as regular inspections, pruning for growth management, and pest control to avert problems before they arise. Both types are essential for the health and longevity of residential trees, enhancing property aesthetics and safety.</p></p>
