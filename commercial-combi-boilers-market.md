<p><p>The commercial combi boilers market is primarily categorized into non-condensing and condensing boilers. Non-condensing boilers operate at lower efficiency, primarily using the heat generated from combustion without recovering exhaust gases' heat. In contrast, condensing boilers maximize energy efficiency by capturing and utilizing the heat from exhaust gases, resulting in lower fuel consumption and greenhouse gas emissions. While non-condensing models are often more affordable, condensing boilers are increasingly favored for their energy savings and environmental benefits in commercial applications.</p></p>
