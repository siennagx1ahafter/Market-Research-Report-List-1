<p><p>The WFI (Water for Injection) Production System encompasses various market types, primarily including Vapor Compression Distillation and Reverse Osmosis combined with Ultrafiltration (UF). Both methods are essential for meeting stringent regulatory standards in the production of parenteral products.</p></p>
