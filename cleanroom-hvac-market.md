<p><p>The Cleanroom HVAC market can be categorized into decentralized and centralized systems. Decentralized systems operate independently in each cleanroom, allowing for precise control over temperature, humidity, and air quality within specific areas. This is ideal for facilities with varied needs. Centralized systems, on the other hand, use a central unit to manage air supply for multiple cleanrooms, offering efficiency in larger operations. Both approaches play crucial roles in maintaining contamination control and compliance in sensitive environments.</p></p>
