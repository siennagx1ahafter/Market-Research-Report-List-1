<p><ul><li>Lubricating Oil for Fluid Suspension</li><li>Independent Suspension Lubricating Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive suspension system lubricants market is primarily divided into two types: lubricating oil for fluid suspension and independent suspension lubricating oil. </p><p>Lubricating oil for fluid suspension is designed to facilitate the movement of fluids within hydraulic suspension systems, providing cushioning and stability. Meanwhile, independent suspension lubricating oil is tailored for systems where each wheel operates independently, enhancing ride quality and handling. Both types are crucial for reducing friction, wear, and ensuring the longevity of suspension components in vehicles.</p></p>
