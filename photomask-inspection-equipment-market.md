<p><strong>The Photomask Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photomask Inspection Equipment market features two primary methods: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB). The Die to Die method inspects individual dies on a photomask, comparing them against one another to identify defects. In contrast, the Die to Database method compares the features of the die against a pre-established database of known good designs, enhancing detection accuracy. Both methods are essential for ensuring high-quality photomasks, facilitating defect management in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
