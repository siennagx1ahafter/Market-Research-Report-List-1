<p><p>The Vehicle Auto Dimming Mirror market comprises two main types: inside and outside auto dimming mirrors. Inside auto dimming mirrors are typically mounted on the windshield and adjust their tint to reduce glare from vehicles behind, enhancing nighttime driving safety. Outside auto dimming mirrors are located on the side mirrors and serve a similar purpose, decreasing glare from headlights of approaching traffic. Both types contribute to improved visibility and comfort for drivers, thereby increasing road safety.</p></p>
