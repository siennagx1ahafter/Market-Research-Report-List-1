<p><p>The antifreeze and coolants market is primarily categorized into three technologies. Inorganic Acid Technology (IAT) utilizes conventional inhibitors, offering shorter service life and is often compatible with older vehicles. Organic Acid Technology (OAT), on the other hand, uses organic compounds, providing longer-lasting protection and is suitable for modern vehicles. Hybrid Organic Acid Technology (HOAT) combines features of both IAT and OAT, offering balanced protection and compatibility with a wide range of vehicles, making it versatile for diverse applications.</p></p>
