<p><p>Leaf and litter vacuums are categorized into three main types based on their power source: electric, diesel, and petrol. Electric vacuums are lightweight, eco-friendly, and ideal for small to medium areas, operating quietly and with low maintenance. Diesel vacuums offer greater power and efficiency for larger commercial tasks but are heavier and noisier. Petrol vacuums are versatile and powerful, suitable for various terrains, making them ideal for both residential and extensive landscaping projects, though they tend to be less environmentally friendly.</p></p>
