<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle Range Extender market features two primary types: water-cooled and air-cooled range extenders. Water-cooled range extenders use liquid cooling systems to manage engine temperature, providing efficient thermal management and enabling longer operational ranges. In contrast, air-cooled range extenders rely on ambient air to dissipate heat, offering a simpler design with fewer components. Both systems enhance the driving range of electric vehicles, catering to different consumer preferences and requirements in terms of design complexity, efficiency, and performance.</p></p>
